What is Leverage?

Leverage involves borrowing capital to increase the size of your trading position. For example, with 10x leverage, you can control £10,000 worth of assets with only £1,000 of your own money. This magnifies your exposure to market movements, potentially boosting your profits.

Leverage is a well-established and institutionalized practice in financial trading. When used with a rigorous risk management strategy, it can significantly enhance profit potential. However, it's crucial to manage leverage carefully to avoid substantial losses.

How Dividends Work
